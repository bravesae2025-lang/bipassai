Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Who we are

Bipass AI is a writing tool that helps you humanize and generate text. This policy explains what data we collect when you use the Bipass AI website and the Bipass AI Auto Typer browser extension.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address and display name, provided when you sign in with Google or email and password.
  • Text results — the humanized or generated text your account produces, stored so you can access your history and use the Auto Typer extension.
  • Authentication tokens — access and refresh tokens stored locally in your browser (via chrome.storage.local for the extension, or session cookies for the website) to keep you signed in.
  • Usage tier — whether your account is on the free or paid plan, stored in your account metadata.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not read the content of pages you visit.
  • The Auto Typer extension only injects into the active tab when you explicitly press Start — it does not run in the background on other pages.

How we use your data

  • To authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • To store and display your text results in History and the extension.
  • To track your credit balance and plan status.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

Third-party services

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your account data and results are stored on Supabase servers. See Supabase's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — used to process payments on the Pro plan. We do not store your card details. See Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Google OAuth — used as an optional sign-in method. We only receive your email and display name.

Data retention

Your text results are kept until you delete them from History. Auth tokens are cleared when you sign out. If you delete your account, all associated data is removed.

Your rights

You can delete your results at any time from the History page. To request full account deletion or a copy of your data, contact us at the address below.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@bipassai.com.
