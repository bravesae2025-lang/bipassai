Why AI writing gets noticed

Raw AI text from ChatGPT or Gemini reads too smooth: even sentence lengths, polished vocabulary, and word choices that are easy to predict. A teacher who's read your past work can feel that it isn't you — and detectors like Turnitin and GPTZero score the exact same smoothness.

Real students don't write like that. They vary sentence length, repeat themselves, use simpler words, and make small mistakes. That natural messiness is what makes writing read human.

FAQ

Sounding Human — Questions

Will my teacher be able to tell it's AI?
Often yes — raw AI writing reads too polished and uniform, and a teacher who knows your work notices. Bipass AI rewrites it to read like a real student wrote it. No tool can promise a teacher won't notice, but text that genuinely reads human is far less likely to stand out.
Does Turnitin detect ChatGPT?
Yes. Turnitin has a dedicated AI writing detector separate from its plagiarism checker. It gives your document an AI percentage based on how predictable and uniform the writing is — and ChatGPT text scores high by default.
Does GPTZero detect ChatGPT?
Yes. GPTZero scores writing on perplexity and burstiness — how predictable your words are and how much your sentence lengths vary. Uniform, predictable AI text reads as AI to it by default.
How does Bipass AI help?
It rewrites your essay to read like a real student wrote it — varying sentence rhythm, swapping over-polished words, and adding the small imperfections a person makes — so it doesn't read like obvious AI. No tool can promise what Turnitin will say, but text that genuinely reads human is far less likely to stand out.
Which level should I use?
For academic essays, the Student level is the most believable. It keeps your meaning intact while adding enough human variation to read naturally. Use Custom if you want to control exactly what changes.
Will it change the meaning of my essay?
No. Bipass AI keeps your argument and points the same — it only adjusts how the text reads. You can review every change before you submit and undo categories you don't want.
Is using Bipass AI against the rules?
Bipass AI is a writing tool. How you use it is your responsibility — always follow your school's academic integrity policy. Many students use it to make their own AI-assisted drafts read in their natural voice.
