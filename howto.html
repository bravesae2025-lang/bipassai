04

Train it on your own writing style

This is the most powerful feature. Paste 1–5 samples of your own writing (essays, messages, anything you wrote yourself) and Bipass AI will analyze your personal quirks — spelling habits, grammar patterns, punctuation style.

Once analyzed, each detected trait shows as a bar you can slide from None → A little → A lot. Toggle Use My Style and every generation will match your specific writing fingerprint, not a generic human voice.

Best practice Use writing samples that you didn't try hard on — casual essays, old assignments, chat messages. The more natural, the better the profile.

FAQ

Common Questions

Does it bypass Turnitin and GPTZero?
Yes. Bipass AI specifically targets the patterns those detectors flag. Hard mode is most effective for academic writing — or use My Style to match your own writing fingerprint.
What are credits?
Credits are used when you Humanize or Generate text. 1 credit = 1 character in the output. Analyzing your style is always free and doesn't cost credits.
Why does it need multiple writing samples?
More samples = better profile. One sample might just reflect the topic's style. Multiple samples let Bipass spot habits that are uniquely yours across different contexts.
Can I use it for any language?
Bipass AI works best with English. It can process other languages but the humanization patterns are optimized for English text and detectors.
What's the difference between Humanize and Generate?
Humanize rewrites text you already have. Generate writes brand new text from a topic or prompt. Both use the same humanization settings and My Style profile.
Is my writing saved anywhere?
Your history is saved to your account so you can review past results. Your style profile is stored to your account. We never use your writing to train any AI model.
